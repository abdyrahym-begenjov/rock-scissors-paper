============================================================
         🕹️  SCISSORS, PAPER, ROCK — GAME RULES  📯
============================================================

Welcome to the ultimate Rock, Paper, Scissors showdown!
Below are the rules and guidelines to help you navigate the 
game.

🎮  HOW TO PLAY
------------------------------------------------------------
1. Start the game and select "Game" from the main menu.
2. Choose the total number of winning points required to win 
   the overall match.
3. In each round, enter your choice: Rock, Scissors, or Paper.


⚔️ THE COMBAT LAWS
------------------------------------------------------------
• 🪨  Rock smashes  ✂️  Scissors (Rock wins)
• ✂️  Scissors cuts  📄  Paper (Scissors wins)
• 📄  Paper covers  🪨  Rock (Paper wins)
• 🤝  Same choices result in a tie/draw (No points awarded)


🏆  MATCH VICTORY & SCORING SYSTEM
------------------------------------------------------------
• The first player (You or the Computer) to reach the chosen 
  number of winning rounds wins the entire game!
• Upon match completion, points are recorded in the highscores:
  - If you win, the chosen number of target points is added to 
    your profile's victories column.
  - If you lose, the chosen number of target points is added to 
    your profile's defeats column.


📊  GAME MODES & MENUS
------------------------------------------------------------
• Game: Start a new match against the computer.
• Rules: View this quick guide to the game's mechanics, 
  winning conditions, and menu navigation.
• Highscores: Check your lifetime wins versus the computer's 
  wins sorted by round types.
• Settings: Change your username or switch your language at 
  any time.
• Exit: Safely close the game application.
