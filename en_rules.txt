============================================================
         🪨  SCISSORS, PAPER, ROCK — GAME RULES  📯
============================================================

Welcome to the ultimate Rock, Paper, Scissors showdown! Below 
are the rules and guidelines to help you navigate the game.

🎮 HOW TO PLAY
------------------------------------------------------------
1. Start the game and select "Game" from the main menu.
2. Choose the total number of winning points required to win 
   the overall match.
3. In each round, enter your choice: Rock, Scissors, or Paper.


⚔️ THE COMBAT LAWS
------------------------------------------------------------
• 🪨 Rock smashes ✂️ Scissors (Rock wins)
• ✂️ Scissors cuts 📄 Paper (Scissors wins)
• 📄 Paper covers 🪨 Rock (Paper wins)
• 🤝 Same choices result in a tie/draw (No points awarded)


🏆 WINNING THE MATCH
------------------------------------------------------------
• The first player (You or the Computer) to reach the chosen 
  number of winning rounds wins the entire game!


📊 GAME MODES & MENUS
------------------------------------------------------------
• Game: Start a new match against the computer.
• Rules: The mode provides players with a quick guide to the game's mechanics, winning conditions, and menu navigation.
• Highscores: Check your lifetime wins versus the computer's wins 
  sorted by round types.
• Settings: Change your username or switch your language at 
  any time.
• Exit: Safely close the game application.
